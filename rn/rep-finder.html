Your Voice Matters

Did you know it only takes seven people to influence a state senator? Seven.

You and six friends or neighbors or co-workers or acquaintances. Seven.

And it doesn't take that long to voice your opinion. Senators can receive emails on the floor of the legislature. They really do read and respond. Just make it genuine, respectful and get to the point. Calls are great, too. And so is traditional contact through letters or face-to-face meetings. The point is, your voice really does matter. Senators really listen. So, take a few minutes and share your opinion.
