RESOURCES

In case you want to geek out on policy, we have a few resources for you to read. These are research-based, independent data analysis on Nebraska's budget, public education finances, as well as interesting state agency topics. Additionally, there is a link to the Legislative Fiscal Office - a great resource for the latest budget information. And the Nebraska Legislature Bill Tracker page.

Budget and Tax Policy Overview

Take a budget dive and learn more about Nebraska's budget and tax policy from OpenSky Policy Institute. Executive Director Renee Fry provides useful, easy-to-understand information.

Corrections Overview

Nebraska's correctional system is experiencing challenges in staffing and facilities. Doug Koebernick, Inspector General of the Nebraska Correctional System, shares a recent report and explains system priorities.

Education Finance Overview

If you're interested in how Nebraska funds K-12 public education, Tiffany Joekel, policy director at OpenSky Policy Institute explains the formula and that Nebraska ranks 49th in the U.S. in terms of state support for public education (the rest is made up from property taxes, which is why local governments rely so heavily on them).

Child Welfare and Juvenile Justice Overview

In a recent about 3,000 children were state wards at any given snapshot of time. Kim Hawekotte, Executive Director of the Nebraska Foster Care Office shares information about what this means for the children and families as well as the impact on our state.
